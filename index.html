About Me

Hi, I am Tom Black, a front-end web developer with a strong focus on building modern, responsive websites. I am passionate about WordPress and enjoy combining it with clean, semantic HTML and well-structured CSS to create user-friendly digital experiences. My work so far has focused on mobile-first design, accessible page structure, and thoughtful layout systems using Flexbox and Grid, while adding polished interaction through transitions and animations. I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am always aiming to improve both usability and visual quality in every project.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Do I need a domain name to get started?

    Not at all. I can help you secure a domain through a trusted provider, or work with your existing domain if you already have one. Whether you are starting fresh or rebuilding, I have got you covered.

  2. Do I need hosting before we begin?

    No. I will guide you through the best hosting options based on your needs and budget. If you already have hosting, I can use that too.

  3. Can you redesign my existing website?

    Absolutely. If your current site feels outdated or underperforms, I can rebuild it into a modern, high-converting design while keeping what works.

  4. How long does it take to build a website?

    Most websites are completed within 5-10 working days, depending on the size and complexity of the project.

  5. Will my website work on mobile phones?

    Yes. Every website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

  6. Can I update the website myself after it is done?

    Definitely. I build user-friendly websites, especially with WordPress, and I can show you exactly how to make updates yourself.

  7. Do you offer eCommerce functionality?

    Yes. I can set up a full online store, including products, payments, and order management using WooCommerce.

  8. Will my website be SEO-friendly?

    Yes. Your website will be built with SEO best practices to help you rank better on Google and attract more traffic.

  9. What do you need from me to get started?

    Just the basics: your business info, any images or content you have, and a general idea of your style. If you do not have content yet, I can help with that too.

  10. Do you offer ongoing support?

    Yes. I offer ongoing support and maintenance to keep your website running smoothly after launch.

  11. What makes your service different?

    I focus on clean, modern design that actually converts visitors into customers. This is not just a pretty website, but one that works for your business.
